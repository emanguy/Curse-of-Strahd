# The Land of Barovia
![[CurseofStrahd_LandOfBarovia_DM.jpg]]

Surrounded by impassable fog. Only the [[Vistani]] can go back and forth. Always cloudy or storming. Normal populous is the [[Barovians]].

* **H** - [[Tser Falls]]
* **N** - [[Vallaki]]

* You can't teleport out of Barovia
