# Jump

- [ ] Homebrew

***1st-level transmutation***

**Casting Time:** action

**Duration:** 1 minute

**Range:** touch

**Tags:** verbal, somatic, material

**Material Components:** a grasshopper's hind leg

---

## Description
You touch a creature.
The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.

**Sources:** PHB, page 254
